FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

What makes your web development different?

+

We build custom, hand-coded websites without using templates or page builders. This ensures faster loading times, better SEO performance, and a truly unique design that matches your brand.

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Most projects take 4-6 weeks from start to finish. This includes the discovery phase, design, development, and revisions. Complex projects may take longer, while simple landing pages can be completed in 2-3 weeks.

Do you work with clients internationally?

+

Yes! We work with clients worldwide and can accommodate different time zones for meetings and communication.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

+

Absolutely! We build all websites with a mobile-first approach, ensuring they look and function perfectly on all devices, from smartphones to desktop computers.

Do you provide hosting services?

+

We host all our websites on GitHub Pages, which provides excellent reliability, security, and performance at no additional cost.

Can I update the website myself?

+

Yes! We provide training and documentation so you can make basic content updates. For more complex changes, our maintenance plans have you covered.

What do you need from me to get started?

+

To begin, we'll need:

  • Your brand assets (logo, colors, etc.)
  • Content for your website
  • Examples of websites you like
  • A clear understanding of your goals

How do revisions work?

+

Each project includes two rounds of revisions. We'll work closely with you during each phase to ensure everything meets your expectations before moving forward.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

+

Yes, we offer flexible maintenance plans that include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and content updates. You can choose the plan that best fits your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Projects typically require a 50% deposit to begin, with the remaining balance due upon completion.

Are there any hidden costs?

+

No hidden costs! Our quotes include everything needed for your website. The only additional costs would be optional maintenance plans or future modifications you request.
