Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | We protect your data with the same care we build websites

Your Privacy Matters

At T&H WebWorks, we treat your privacy like we treat our code - with care, security, and attention to detail. Here's exactly what information we collect and why:

Information We Collect

We only collect what we need to build your amazing website:

  • Contact details when you reach out (name, email, phone)
  • Project information to understand your needs
  • Business details to create relevant content
  • Website analytics to improve our services
  • Payment information for billing (securely processed)

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us deliver exceptional service:

  • Respond to your inquiries quickly and personally
  • Build and deliver your custom website
  • Send project updates and important communications
  • Provide ongoing support and maintenance
  • Improve our services based on feedback
  • Comply with legal and tax requirements

We Don't Sell Your Data

Period. We never sell, rent, or trade your information. We only share data when:

  • You give us explicit permission
  • We need trusted partners to deliver your project (like hosting providers)
  • Required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Part of a business merger (you'd be notified)

Fort Knox-Level Security

We protect your data like it's our own source code:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL certificates)
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited team access on a need-to-know basis
  • Industry-standard security practices

Website Analytics & Cookies

We use analytics to make our website better (just like we optimize yours!):

  • Page views and user behavior (anonymous data)
  • Device and browser information for compatibility
  • Geographic data for service improvement
  • Performance metrics to optimize loading speeds

Cookies help us remember your preferences and provide a better experience. You can disable them, but some features might not work perfectly.

Third-Party Tools & Services

To build amazing websites, we sometimes use trusted third-party services:

  • Hosting providers for reliable website performance
  • Analytics tools to understand how sites perform
  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Email services for communication

We only work with reputable providers who meet our security standards.

Client Project Confidentiality

When you hire us, your project details are locked down tight:

  • All project information remains strictly confidential
  • We sign NDAs when requested
  • Project files are stored securely and access-controlled
  • We only keep data as long as needed for support
  • Your business secrets stay secret

Your Data, Your Control

You're in complete control of your information:

  • Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications
  • Object to how we process your data

Just email us and we'll take care of it within 48 hours.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active project data: Duration of project + 2 years for support
  • Contact information: Until you ask us to remove it
  • Analytics data: Anonymized after 26 months
  • Financial records: 7 years for tax compliance

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as our services evolve. When we do:

  • We'll notify you via email for major changes
  • Updates will be posted on our website
  • The "effective date" will be updated
  • Continued use means you accept the changes

Questions About Privacy?

  • Response time: Within 24 hours

Your privacy is protected. Your data is secure. Your trust is earned. Ready to build something amazing?
